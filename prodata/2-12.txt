The omnipresent computer has become the backbone of our technological society, fundamentally altering how we navigate the intricacies of our daily lives. In the digital age, the term computer transcends mere hardware; it encapsulates a dynamic force that permeates every facet of our existence. This article delves into the profound impact of computers on our world, exploring their historical evolution, intricate components, and the ever-expanding horizons they unveil.The fascinating journey of the computer traces its roots to the early 20th century, marked by the advent of mechanical calculating devices. However, it was the emergence of electronic computers, exemplified by the monumental ENIAC, that catapulted these machines from calculating tools to sophisticated data processors.
