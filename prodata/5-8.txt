Narrative complexity within games has evolved into a storytelling art form. From branching storylines in role-playing games to emotionally impactful narratives in adventure games, storytelling in gaming has become a dynamic and participatory experience. Players' choices and interactions shape the unfolding narratives, providing a level of immersion and agency that distinguishes gaming as a unique storytelling medium.The rise of game streaming platforms and content creation has transformed gaming into a spectator sport. Platforms like Twitch and YouTube Gaming provide a space for gamers to broadcast their gameplay, share insights, and entertain global audiences. Content creators, from streamers to video essayists, contribute to the gaming community's discourse, shaping trends, and influencing gaming culture. 5
