Indie games, developed by smaller, independent studios, have emerged as influential contributors to the gaming landscape. These titles often showcase innovative gameplay mechanics, artistic creativity, and unique storytelling. Platforms like Steam and indie showcases at gaming conventions provide visibility to indie developers, allowing their creations to reach global audiences and challenging the conventions of mainstream game design.The relationship between gaming and mental health is an area of growing interest. While excessive gaming may pose challenges, studies suggest that gaming, in moderation, can have positive effects on cognitive skills, problem-solving abilities, and stress relief. Game developers are increasingly exploring the potential therapeutic benefits of gaming experiences, such as relaxation apps and games designed to support mental health awareness. 5
