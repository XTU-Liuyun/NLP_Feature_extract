Online multiplayer platforms have become virtual social hubs, connecting gamers across the globe. From the collaborative gameplay of massively multiplayer online (MMO) games to the competitive spirit of battle royales, online gaming communities provide spaces for camaraderie and competition. Voice chat, in-game messaging, and social media integration further enhance the social dynamics within gaming, fostering friendships and rivalries that extend beyond the confines of virtual worlds.Gaming platforms, ranging from traditional consoles like PlayStation and Xbox to the flexibility of PC gaming and the mobility of handheld devices, cater to diverse player preferences. The advent of cloud gaming services, such as Google Stadia and Xbox Cloud Gaming, offers new dimensions of accessibility, allowing players to stream and play games on various devices with minimal hardware requirements. The intersection of gaming and technology continues to break down barriers, ensuring that gaming experiences are increasingly accessible to a global audience. 6
